# RES-006: The High-Performance UI Landscape

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Sinta Design Director

---

## 1. Direct Competitor Patterns (The "Interview Standard")

### **Ashby (The "Speed" Benchmark)**
*   **Pattern:** "Command-K" everything. Zero mouse-travel for recruiters.
*   **Sinta Takeaway:** Every major action in Sinta needs a keyboard shortcut. We aren't building a website; we're building a "Pro Tool."
*   **UX Lesson:** Density is a feature, not a bug, when the user is an expert.

### **BrightHire (The "Evidence" Benchmark)**
*   **Pattern:** Transcription-to-Scorecard "Sync."
*   **Sinta Takeaway:** Clicking a "Signal Tag" on our **Review Timeline** must instantly transport the video and transcript to that millisecond. The connection must feel "Physical."
*   **UX Lesson:** Co-located context (Three Columns) reduces the "Cognitive Fetch" cost.

### **Pillar (The "Coaching" Benchmark)**
*   **Pattern:** Live "Interviewer Coaching" prompts.
*   **Sinta Takeaway:** During the **Live Interview**, the "Reflex Bar" should subtly highlight the next competency to cover.
*   **UX Lesson:** The tool is a "Co-pilot," not just a recorder.

---

## 2. Adjacent High-Performance Patterns (The "Expert" Inspiration)

### **Linear (The "Systemic Clarity" Inspiration)**
*   **Pattern:** Subtle 1px borders, semantic background shifts, and high-resolution typography (Satoshi/Inter).
*   **Sinta Takeaway:** **"Tufte-Dark" palette.** No heavy shadows or rounded corners. The UI should "Recede" into the background so the "Candidate Evidence" pops.
*   **UX Lesson:** "To clarify, add detail" (Tufte). Don't simplify by hiding; simplify by structuring.

### **Raycast (The "Command-First" Inspiration)**
*   **Pattern:** Deeply nested but easily navigable lists with "Action Panels."
*   **Sinta Takeaway:** The **Builder**'s "Split-Pane Architecture." Left for the "Tree" (The Pipeline), Right for the "Details" (The Stage Anatomy).
*   **UX Lesson:** Maintain the "Mental Map" on the left while providing the "Working Space" on the right.

### **Vercel (The "Deployment Visuals" Inspiration)**
*   **Pattern:** High-fidelity "Status" indicators and clean "Progress" animations.
*   **Sinta Takeaway:** The **"Reaction Timeline"** should feel like a Vercel deployment graph—alive, accurate, and high-resolution.
*   **UX Lesson:** Trust is built through visual precision.

---

## 3. Sinta's Unique Synthesis: The "Decision Lab"
Sinta is the intersection of **Ashby's Speed**, **BrightHire's Evidence**, and **Linear's Clarity**. We are building a "High-Resolution Decision Machine."
