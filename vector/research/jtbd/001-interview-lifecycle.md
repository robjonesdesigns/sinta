# JTBD-001: The Interview Lifecycle

**Date:** 2026-04-12
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Rob Jones (Design Director)

---

## The Core Struggle

"I need to evaluate this candidate fairly and thoroughly, but the act of interviewing is so cognitively demanding that I lose the thread of the conversation while trying to capture the data."

---

## Job #1: The Architect (Before)
**Situation:** A hiring manager or recruiter is opening a new role.
**Struggle:** Designing a fair, consistent process that doesn't require reinventing the wheel for every candidate.
**Progress:** "When I open a role, I want to snap together a proven interview pipeline so I can start sourcing immediately with confidence that we're measuring the right things."
**Sinta Solution:** The **Builder** flow with modular, draggable stages and template presets.

---

## Job #2: The Facilitator (During)
**Situation:** An interviewer is in a 45-minute live call with a candidate.
**Struggle:** Capturing structured feedback (competencies, red flags, great answers) without breaking eye contact or making the candidate feel like they're being "audited."
**Progress:** "When I'm in the room (or on the screen), I want to tag key moments with one tap so I can stay present in the conversation while building a data-rich record for later."
**Sinta Solution:** The **Live Interview** flow with shortcut buttons and minimal UI distraction.

---

## Job #3: The Jury (After)
**Situation:** The interview is over, and the interviewer needs to submit a scorecard.
**Struggle:** Relying on fuzzy memory or scribbled, out-of-context notes to make a high-stakes "Hire/No Hire" decision.
**Progress:** "When I review a candidate, I want to see the video, the transcript, and my live tags in a single view so I can make an evidence-based decision without second-guessing my memory."
**Sinta Solution:** The **Three-Column Review** flow with co-located evidence.

---

## Job #4: The Decider (Final)
**Situation:** A hiring manager is looking at four final candidates.
**Struggle:** Comparing candidates across different interviewers and different rounds to find the best fit.
**Progress:** "When it's time to make an offer, I want to see a high-resolution comparison of all candidate data so I can justify the hire to the leadership team with objective proof."
**Sinta Solution:** The **Scorecard/Analytics** flow with aggregated competency grids.

---

## Desired Outcomes (Success Metrics)

- **Interviewer Engagement:** Increase in time spent on eye contact vs. looking at notes.
- **Decision Speed:** Reduction in time-to-submit scorecard post-interview.
- **Evaluation Quality:** Higher correlation between interview scores and 90-day performance.
- **Bias Reduction:** More mentions of specific "evidence" in feedback vs. "gut feel."
