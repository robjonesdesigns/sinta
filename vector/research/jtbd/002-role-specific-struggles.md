# JTBD-002: Role-Specific Struggles

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Sinta Lead Architect

## 1. Job: The "Impact Finder" (Product)
**Situation:** A PM is interviewing a Senior PM.
**Struggle:** Distinguishing between a "Feature-Factory PM" and an "Outcome-Driven PM."
**Progress:** "When I ask a 'What happened next?' question, I want to see a visual marker of their 'Value-First' intuition so I can compare it across other candidates."
**Sinta Solution:** "Outcome Focus" signal tag.

## 2. Job: The "Systemic Evaluator" (Design)
**Situation:** A Head of Design is doing a portfolio critique.
**Struggle:** Determining if the designer can "Scale Down" their craft to a modular system.
**Progress:** "When I offer a critique, I want to tag their 'Craft Adaptation' speed so I know if they can flourish in our design-system-first environment."
**Sinta Solution:** "Systemic Thinking" signal tag.

## 3. Job: The "Resilience Scouter" (GTM)
**Situation:** A Sales Manager is doing a "Mock Call" stage.
**Struggle:** Seeing if the AE can "Pivot the Narrative" under pressure.
**Progress:** "When I throw a curveball, I want to mark the 'Pivot Point' on the timeline so I can show the VP exactly why this candidate didn't break."
**Sinta Solution:** "Objection Pivot" signal tag.

## 4. Job: The "Efficiency Mapper" (Ops)
**Situation:** An Ops Lead is asking about a past process failure.
**Struggle:** Seeing if the candidate "Solves with Scale" or "Solves with Headcount."
**Progress:** "When they explain their solution, I want to tag the 'Automation Bias' so I can ensure they won't just ask for more people every time we grow."
**Sinta Solution:** "Scalability Lens" signal tag.

---

## Shared Desire: The "Defensible Hire"
Across all these roles, the common thread is **"Defensibility."** 
"When my CEO asks me why I chose X, I want to show a 'Signal Heatmap' that makes the choice undeniable."
