# PERS-003: Multi-Role Tech Personas (Full Spectrum)

**Date:** 2026-04-12
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Rob Jones (Design Director)

---

## 1. The DevOps / SRE Lead (The "Reliability" Interviewer)
**Goal:** Verifying system-design thinking and "incident-response" calm.
**Pain Point:** Evaluating how a candidate thinks through a cascading failure without a "sandbox" or clear way to mark the candidate's "logic leaps."
**Sinta Solution:** A "Logic Leap" tag in the **Live Interview** to mark where a candidate showed exceptional troubleshooting intuition.

## 2. The QA / SDET Lead (The "Edge-Case" Specialist)
**Goal:** Finding engineers who obsess over what *could* go wrong.
**Pain Point:** Tracking the candidate's "bug-hunt" methodology during a live code review.
**Sinta Solution:** A "Scenario Builder" in the **Builder** specifically for QA, focusing on "Test Coverage Strategy" stages.

## 3. The Product Marketing Manager (The "Narrative" Evaluator)
**Goal:** Assessing how a candidate turns features into value stories.
**Pain Point:** The "Interview Tax" of sitting through 60-minute presentations and trying to remember the *specific* slide where the candidate lost the audience.
**Sinta Solution:** Visual **Reaction Heatmaps** on the **Review** timeline showing where the interviewers "tuned in" or "tuned out."

## 4. The Engineering Manager (The "Force Multiplier")
**Goal:** Finding "Seniors" who mentor and level up the team, not just "Code Ninjas."
**Pain Point:** Evaluating "Mentorship" and "Conflict Resolution" is historically subjective and "gut-based."
**Sinta Solution:** **Behavioral Matrix** scorecards that require evidence-linked ratings from the transcript.
