# PERS-002: Expanded Interviewer Personas (2026 Focus)

**Date:** 2026-04-12
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Rob Jones (Design Director)

---

## 1. The Design Principal (The "Why" Seeker)
**Goal:** Identifying designers who can solve business problems, not just "paint pixels."
**Cognitive Load:** Struggling to track the candidate's rationale while viewing complex Figma prototypes.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Inconsistent evaluation of "soft skills" (storytelling, critique handling) across the interview panel.
**Desired Outcome:** A way to timestamp specific moments of "Product Thinking" or "Conflict Resolution" during a live portfolio walkthrough.

## 2. The Strategic PM Lead (The "Business Judgment" Evaluator)
**Goal:** Assessing a candidate's ability to handle messy, real-world trade-offs and "AI-native" workflows.
**Cognitive Load:** Distinguishing between a "rehearsed" framework answer and genuine strategic insight.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Losing the thread of a complex market-moat argument while trying to write a detailed note.
**Desired Outcome:** One-tap "Insight" vs "Framework" buttons to tag the quality of the answer in real-time.

## 3. The Enterprise CS Director (The "Revenue" Hunter)
**Goal:** Finding CSMs who can drive NRR (Net Revenue Retention) in high-stakes, industry-specific accounts.
**Cognitive Load:** Balancing the need for "warmth/rapport" with the need to audit the candidate's revenue-focused experience.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Evaluating the candidate's "situational EQ" without a clear way to record the nuance of the interaction.
**Desired Outcome:** Real-time sentiment tagging (e.g., "Handled Tension Well," "Clear Articulation of Value") during role-play scenarios.

## 4. The Talent Ops Architect (The "Process" Guardian)
**Goal:** Scaling a consistent, fair, and fast hiring machine across 15+ concurrent roles.
**Cognitive Load:** Managing "recruitment fatigue" and bias across a diverse group of human interviewers.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Chasing "patchy" feedback that relies on gut feel rather than objective evidence.
**Desired Outcome:** A dashboard that surfaces "Evidence Gaps" in real-time (e.g., "No one has evaluated this candidate on 'Ethics' yet").
