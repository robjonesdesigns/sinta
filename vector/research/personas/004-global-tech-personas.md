# PERS-004: Global Tech Personas (Full Spectrum)

**Date:** 2026-04-13
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Sinta Lead Architect

---

## 1. The "Vision-to-Value" Product Manager (PM)
**Goal:** Finding the "Pragmatic Visionary"—someone who can say "no" to a great idea to ship a better outcome.
**The Signal:** "Decision Logic." How do they prioritize under resource constraints?
**The Pain:** Most tools treat PM interviews like storytelling sessions. The PM interviewer needs to see the *math* and *framework* behind the candidate's answers.
**Sinta Implementation:** "Prioritization Framework" signal tags in the Live Interview.

## 2. The "Systems-First" Product Designer (UX/UI)
**Goal:** Identifying designers who understand that "pixels are the byproduct, systems are the product."
**The Signal:** "Critique Reflex." How do they handle immediate, objective feedback on their work?
**The Pain:** Portfolio reviews are often "rehearsed theater." The interviewer needs a way to tag "Systemic Thinking" vs. "Pattern Following" during live whiteboarding.
**Sinta Implementation:** "Critique Loop" markers on the Review Timeline.

## 3. The "High-Velocity" Sales/GTM Lead (Account Executive)
**Goal:** Finding the "Relentless Navigator"—someone who treats an objection as a data point, not a rejection.
**The Signal:** "Objection Resilience." How many "No's" does it take to break their narrative flow?
**The Pain:** Sales interviews lack a "Pressure Metric."
**Sinta Implementation:** "Resilience Marker" heatmaps showing the candidate's recovery speed after a difficult question.

## 4. The "Engine-Room" Ops Specialist (RevOps/BizOps)
**Goal:** Finding the "Scale Architect"—someone who builds a process so they don't have to build a team.
**The Signal:** "Efficiency Intuition." Do they solve problems with headcount or with automation?
**The Pain:** Ops interviews are often buried in spreadsheets; the interviewer needs to see the candidate's "Process Architecture" in real-time.
**Sinta Implementation:** "Automation vs. Manual" signal tagging.

## 5. The "Brand-Narrative" Marketing Lead (PMM)
**Goal:** Finding the "Story-Architect"—someone who can turn a feature list into a movement.
**The Signal:** "Emotional Resonance." Where did the candidate lose the audience during the presentation?
**The Pain:** Hard to quantify "engagement" across a 60-minute pitch.
**Sinta Implementation:** "Tuned-In/Tuned-Out" heatmap overlays on the video player.
