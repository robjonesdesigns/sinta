# PERS-001: The Hiring Team Personas

**Date:** 2026-04-12
**Status:** DRAFT
**Author:** Rob Jones (Design Director)

---

## 1. The Engineering Director (Hiring Manager)
**Goal:** Fill the role with the highest-quality talent while minimizing the engineering team's "interview tax."
**Traits:** Technical, skeptical of "fluff," values evidence over gut feel, short on time.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Seeing conflicting feedback from interviewers with no proof to resolve the debate.
**Use Case:** Designs the initial **Builder** pipeline and reviews the final **Scorecard** for high-stakes decisions.

## 2. The Senior Engineer (Interviewer)
**Goal:** Conduct a thorough technical evaluation without losing the thread of the conversation.
**Traits:** Deeply focused, high-precision, easily distracted by "noisy" UI, hates busywork.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Forgetting a key technical detail or "red flag" by the time they get around to writing the feedback.
**Use Case:** Uses the **Live Interview** flow to tag competencies and timestamps in real-time.

## 3. The Recruiter / Ops (The Architect)
**Goal:** Ensure every candidate has a consistent experience and every role has a clean pipeline.
**Traits:** Process-oriented, organizes the mess, high volume (manages 15+ roles at once).
**Sinta Pain Point:** Manually chasing interviewers for scorecards and duct-taping data together across Zoom and Google Docs.
**Use Case:** Uses the **Builder** to standardize pipelines and the **Scheduling** flow to manage candidate flow.

## 4. The HR Leader (Compliance & Equity)
**Goal:** Ensure the hiring process is fair, defensible, and free of systemic bias.
**Traits:** Strategic, protective of the company's culture and legal standing, focused on data trends.
**Sinta Pain Point:** Having no "paper trail" for why a candidate was rejected beyond a simple "not a culture fit."
**Use Case:** Reviews the **Analytics** and **Review** flows to audit hiring decisions and train interviewers.
