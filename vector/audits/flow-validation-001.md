## 1. The 6-Screen Hypothesis

We are building for a **High-Resolution Hiring Machine**. Here is the validation of the flow:

### **Screen 1: Dashboard (The "Hiring Pulse")**
*   **Verdict:** VALID.
*   **Pivot:** Move away from "Candidate Pipeline" and toward **"Hiring Health."** Show me which stages are "Low Signal" and where candidates are "Ghosting."

### **Screen 2: Builder (The "Signal Architect")**
*   **Verdict:** VALID.
*   **Pivot:** It must be more than a "Sequence Manager." It's where the **EM**, **PM**, and **Design Head** define the **"Signals"** (Logic Leap, Objection Pivot, Critique Reflex) that the rest of the app will hunt for.
*   **UI Pattern:** Use the **"Split-Pane Architecture"** (Raycast-style) to handle high-density configuration.

### **Screen 3: Scheduling (The "Frictionless Bridge")**
*   **Verdict:** VALID.
*   **Pivot:** Focus on **"Time-to-Offer."** Automate the "Prep-Brief" for candidates so they feel "Invested" before they even join the call.

### **Screen 4: Live Interview (The "Zero-Latency Reflex")**
*   **Verdict:** VALID.
*   **Pivot:** **The "Reflex Bar."** A floating, semi-transparent UI of the signals defined in the Builder. One-tap tagging. Eye contact is the priority.
*   **UI Pattern:** Dark Mode is mandatory here to reduce distraction from the candidate's video feed.

### **Screen 5: Review / Decision Lab (The "Tufte Evidence Engine")**
*   **Verdict:** VALID.
*   **Pivot:** This is the most critical screen. **Three-Column Layout.** Video (Left) + Evidence/Notes (Center) + Transcript/Verdict (Right). Everything is physically linked.
*   **UI Pattern:** **"Sync-Scroll."** Scrolling one column updates the others.

### **Screen 6: Scorecard / Verdict (The "Defensible Proof")**
*   **Verdict:** VALID.
*   **Pivot:** Focus on **"Cross-Candidate Comparison."** Show me how the "Signal Density" of Candidate A compares to Candidate B across all interviewers.

## 2. Global Tech Adaptability (Validation)
Does this flow work for **PM**, **Design**, and **GTM** roles?
*   **Yes:** Because the "Stages" are just containers for "Signals." The PM Lead can build a "Strategic Vision" signal set, while the SRE Lead builds a "Troubleshooting" signal set. 
*   **The Sinta "Genius" Point:** The product doesn't change for different roles; the **"Signal Doctrine"** does.
